    Is it worth using a Recruitment agency?

    Simply imagine, you're seeking for the assistance of someone who is aware and who is a professional. Recruitment Agencies cooperate directly with Employer companies and usually they have a much better understanding of specifics what an Employer is searching for.


    Besides, they most probably know of Vacant Roles that you'd find difficult to find and learn about on your own. Very often, that many Vacant Roles are not published in the internet or Job platforms. What is more, Agency can also support you in becoming a better candidate and increase your chances of getting a Job.

    Do I have to pay for Recruitment agency support? Is there a fee?

    Most often all costs and fees are covered by Employers who provide Vacancies and Jobs. But, sometimes Job Seeker might be asked to cover some expenses connected with the recruitment procedure or documents set up after a positive interview for example. In any case, we always try to make it absolutely free for Candidates, so they can just mainly concentrate themselves on interview preparation.


    Speaking in general, it is much more common nowadays, that Employers tend to foot the bill and all expenses connected with recruitment.
